The Best Movies According to Ravi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Wolf of Wall Street (2013)

Directed by Martin Scorsese, this biographical black comedy stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Jordan Belfort, a stockbroker who engages in corruption and fraud on Wall Street. The film showcases Belfort's rise and fall, highlighting the excesses of the financial industry in the 1990s.

Margin Call (2011)

Set in the early stages of the 2008 financial crisis, this thriller follows key people at an investment bank as they discover the extent of their financial problems. The film delves into moral dilemmas and corporate greed, featuring a strong ensemble cast, including Kevin Spacey, Paul Bettany, and Jeremy Irons.

The Big Short (2015)

Based on Michael Lewis's book, this film tells the story of the financial crisis of 2007-2008 through the eyes of several outsiders who predicted the collapse of the housing market and bet against it. With a star-studded cast including Christian Bale, Steve Carell, and Ryan Gosling, the film uses humor and innovative storytelling to explain complex financial concepts.
